The essence

Body and mind are not separate systems requiring separate disciplines. They are one living reality — and healing must meet them as such.

Path to Health is an integrative medical practice in Cape Town where medicine and psychotherapy work as one. For fifty years, Dr. Raoul Goldberg has combined European clinical precision with the quiet authority of earth-bound healing — hand-compounded botanicals, rhythmical preparation, nature in the room.

Most integrative clinics treat body and mind as adjacent specialisms. Path to Health treats them as a single field of inquiry — using PATH Method as both a diagnostic and a therapeutic tool to find the often-hidden roots of physical symptoms, then engaging body and mind together to reach what conventional medicine alone cannot.

An extension of Western medicine, in Steiner’s terms — not an alternative.

01 · Brand Foundation

Three entities, one lineage.

A practitioner, a place, and a method — each with a distinct job, bound by a single philosophy of care.

The practitioner

Dr. Raoul Goldberg

Integrative physician & psychotherapist, 50 years’ practice. Swiss- and German-trained, anthroposophic lineage, PhD. The lineage holder and author — whose work the brand must be able to outlive.

The place

Path to Health Centre

Brick-and-mortar centre in Constantia, Cape Town. The validator and proving ground — where the Method is tried, proven, taught, and birthed. Medicine, dispensary, garden, and teaching under one roof.

The method

PATH Method

A psycho-somatic, whole-person modality — the intellectual property. Both a diagnostic and a therapeutic tool. Designed to be teachable, licensable, and to endure beyond any one practitioner.

Core values

  • How can we help you help yourself?
  • Everyone is welcome.
  • A holistic view of the human being — body, soul, and spirit in relationship to the world.
  • Growth and creativity are part of healing.
  • I, You, We — participatory, relational, co-created.

The promise to every patient

You will be seen, heard, and supported here. We wish to activate your capacity to heal yourself.
The work, in one moment
“I need to see what your inner physician looks like, so I can partner with that part of you.” Dr. Raoul Goldberg · the opening of every consult

The brand’s spine is co-creation. Raoul finds the empowered self — the inner physician — and partners with it to design the journey. He moves the patient from victim to agency; from that presence, a different kind of medicine becomes possible. On the body side, he makes decisions with you. On the psyche side, PATH Method tends the hurt parts so the body need not recreate the illness.

02 · Positioning & Differentiation

The spine, and two wedges.

The integration of medical treatment and psychotherapy under one roof is the brand’s spine. Each entity then drives its own distinct wedge into the market.

Centre wedge

Swiss medicine, Cape Town soul

European integrative medicine at South African rates, with a combination of treatments not available together elsewhere — Heckel hyperthermia, Inuspheresis, ozone, IV nutrition, PRP/stem-cell, and integrative psychotherapy under one roof — inside a warm, wood-and-garden environment patients describe with one word: wow.

+

Method wedge

No therapist required

Once the choreography is learned, PATH Method is self-applicable — in the car, at home, in the supermarket — unlike IFS, Somatic Experiencing, or Hakomi, which need a facilitator in the room. And it goes further: beyond witnessing into resourcing the hurt one, casting a remedial inner character that other modalities don’t include.

What sets us apart

  • Medicine + psychotherapy as a single field, not adjacent specialisms
  • PATH Method as both diagnostic and therapeutic
  • Old-school apothecary: precision-compounded botanicals
  • Unique treatment combination under one roof
  • A physical space that does the brand work already

The proof

  • 50 years of integrated practice
  • 100% referral — no marketing, fully booked
  • 51-patient HRV trial measuring the Method in real time
  • PhD (2021) researching the Method on stress
  • 10+ years teaching at Esalen and worldwide
  • Multigenerational families — three generations of patients

What we are not

  • Not biohacking or “bulletproof” wellness culture
  • Not New Age, stone-circle, or “ancient wisdom”
  • Not cold European clinical-spa
  • Not a personality cult around Raoul
  • Not German New Medicine or pseudoscience
  • Not a digital product masquerading as a clinic

On “not yet published”

Many treatments aren’t in major journals — not because they don’t work, but because they’re too affordable to fund trials. Mistletoe has been used 60+ years; red-light for 15; hyperthermia for 40. The framing for an international audience: these treatments are ahead of the curve and clinically demonstrated over decades, not risky — they simply haven’t been studied enough to appear in journals. They pose no risk to patients.

03 · Audiences

Two doors, many people.

The Centre and the Method each gather distinct audiences. The website is the validator — not a lead-gen tool — giving global polish to credibility when people search after Oxford or the book.

Path to Health Centre

  • Local, general health — multi-generational families. ~70% of business; the bread and butter.
  • Chronically & critically ill — MS, fibromyalgia, stage-4 cancer. Seeking integrative care and to be seen as a person, not a case.
  • Swallows & nomads — wealthy, often European, 5–6 months a year in Cape Town. Beauty, performance, longevity, Inuspheresis. Aspirational, not yet actual.
  • The apothecary — not an audience but a brand pillar: the bridge between earth-bound roots and cutting-edge medicine.
  • Holistic beauty — the future, post-Raoul growth vector (Ivy Life).

PATH Method

  • Practitioners — MDs, psychologists, nurses, social workers. Need CEs, billing compatibility, evidence. Enter via pathmethod.com, validated by the Centre.
  • Coaches & HR — organisational and individual; conflict resolution, team dynamics.
  • Well-being professionals — somatic therapists, yoga, breathwork. The Esalen audience.
  • Seekers — burnout, anxiety, transition. Looking to find power within, not be told what to do.
  • Creatives — writers, architects. Not a moneymaker, but a powerful testimonial source.

The bridge between practitioner and seeker is thin — many practitioners are also seekers, a few steps ahead, able to lead others.

04 · Entity Architecture

Peers, not parent and child.

The Method and the Centre are equals. The Centre is the proving ground; the Method is intellectual property held in a new entity that licenses to practitioners and partners. The Method must outlive its founder — as Five Rhythms outlived Gabrielle Roth, and Jungian work outlived Jung.

Proving ground

Path to Health Centre

Cape Town. Brick-and-mortar. The entry portal that validates the digital — medical services, dispensary, education, and space rentals, anchored by Dr. Raoul and Dana Smirin.

↔

The IP

PATH Method

Global reach. A new entity holds the IP and licenses it — professional training, online courses, workshops, and retreats. pathmethod.com carries 14 years of equity worth keeping.

Dr. Raoul sits across both as lineage holder and author — The Trauma You Call Normal (Whitefox, Oct 2026) cross-references each without belonging to either.

05 · Voice & Messaging

Steady, warm, invitational.

Fifty years of practice gives the right to speak slowly. The voice should do what the clinic does: calm you without losing rigour.

Attribute 01Steady

Not urgent, not trendy, not selling. Confidence without performance — someone who has seen thousands of patients and doesn’t need to convince you. Consistency is what makes people feel safe.

Sounds like: “We combine medical care and psychotherapy under one roof, because chronic illness often begins in the psyche.”

Attribute 02Warm but precise

Medical accuracy, human warmth. No jargon walls, no fluff. Calm rather than soften; welcoming rather than warm. The precision is real — each remedy hand-compounded for where the patient is presenting now.

Sounds like: “Dr. Raoul compounds each remedy by hand from raw botanicals, in the rhythmical tradition of anthroposophic pharmacy.”

Attribute 03Invitational

The voice extends an invitation, not a claim. It asks you to participate in your own healing — never prescriptive, never passive. You are not handing over your agency; you are stepping into your own centre, and the doctor meets you there.

Sounds like: “Are you willing to meet the part of yourself that already knows how to heal?”

06 · Visual Direction — Convergence

Warm ground, light spectrum.

The brand holds two poles — the warm, grounded apothecary and the etheric light-spectrum — in one system. The light lives in Cape Town sunset pigment, not screen rainbow; the warmth keeps it from ever tipping cold.

The threefold, with a heart

Raoul’s three circles, kept as concept and freed from the stale diagram: three overlapping circles whose edges carve a heart at the centre — the trinity of body, mind, and nature, with care at the core. Where they meet, the overlaps can combine into colour: primary light into infinite potential.

07 · Where We Stand

Settled, and still open.

What the brand has locked, and the decisions still on the table for this team to make.

Settled

  • Names final — “Path to Health” (Centre) and “PATH Method”
  • “Centre,” not “Clinic” · canonical domain pathtohealthcenter.com
  • Method & Centre are peers; Method IP in a new entity
  • Move fully off anthroposophic pastels — no pastels
  • Cape Town context honoured — present, not colonising
  • Integrative as an extension of medicine, never “alternative”
  • Logo redesign & trademark filing can proceed

Open for the team

  • Lock the visual direction → unblock the design system

  • Entity to hold the Method IP — legal structure
  • Medical-tourism positioning: aspirational vs. who walks in
